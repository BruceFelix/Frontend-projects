Here's a time line of Dr. Borlaug's life:

  • 1976 - Chadwick Aron Boseman was born and raised in Anderson, South Carolina.
  • 1995 - Graduatd from T.L. Hanna High Schoool
  • 1998 - Attends Oxford Summer Program of the British American Drama Academy at Balliol College, Oxford, in England.
  • 2000 - Graduates with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in directing from Howard University in Washington, D.C.
  • 2000-2007 - Theater, Deep Azure, and early television.
  • 2008 - Boseman Moved to Los Angeles to pursue his film and acting career.
  • 2010 - Landed his first regular role in the Persons Unknown as Marine Graham McNair.
  • 2013 - Boseman's second short film as director, Heaven, premiered as the HollyShorts Film Festival.
  • 2014 - Boseman starred in another sporting film, Draft Day.
  • 2016 - Boseman began portraying the Marvel Comics character T'Challa/Black Panther.
  • 2018 - Boseman returned as the Black Panther in 2018.
  • 2019 - He reprised the role in both Avengers: Infinity War and Avengers: Endgame.
  • 2019 - He starred in 21 Bridges.
  • 2020 - Died from stage IV colon cancer.
